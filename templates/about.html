About AI Resume Analyzer

The most intelligent resume optimization platform powered by Google's advanced AI. Created to help professionals succeed in their careers.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe talented professionals deserve better than the "black box" of Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). Our mission is to democratize resume optimization by giving job seekers clear, actionable insights into how their resumes perform against real job requirements.

How Our Technology Works

01

Advanced PDF Parsing

We use industry-leading PDF extraction techniques to convert your resume into structured, clean text. This ensures 100% accuracy in processing all resume formats.

02

Semantic Vector Analysis

Both your resume and job description are converted into mathematical vectors using state-of-the-art sentence transformers. We then calculate cosine similarity to determine your ATS match percentage.

03

Intelligent Data Extraction

Google's Gemini AI extracts and structures key information from your resume including name, contact details, skills, and certifications.

04

Gap Analysis & Recommendations

We identify missing keywords, required skills you don't have, and opportunities to strengthen your candidacy with personalized recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the ATS score?

Our ATS score is based on semantic similarity calculations, which are highly accurate. However, real ATS systems vary. Our score represents your best-case scenario for matching the target job description.

Is my resume data stored?

No. All analysis happens in real-time. Your resume is processed on-the-fly and deleted immediately after analysis. We prioritize your privacy and don't store any personal data.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support PDF files. We recommend saving your resume as PDF from Word or Google Docs for best results on all systems.

How long does analysis take?

Most analyses complete in 30-60 seconds. If the AI service is experiencing high demand, it may take up to 2 minutes due to automatic retries for reliability.

Can I use the same resume for multiple jobs?

Yes! You can analyze the same resume against different job descriptions. We recommend tailoring your resume for each position to maximize your ATS score for that specific role.

What if I get a low ATS score?

Our analysis provides specific recommendations to improve your score. Focus on adding the recommended keywords and skills, then re-analyze to track your progress and see improvement.
